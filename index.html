About me

I’m a Product & UX/UI Designer who loves turning ideas into experiences that feel intuitive, meaningful, and a little bit magical. I thrive at the intersection of creativity and problem-solving, whether it’s designing digital platforms that connect communities, crafting engaging user journeys, or exploring innovative ways to showcase rural craftsmanship and local stories.

I’m naturally curious and always seeking the facts, looking for ways to understand the world better and improve the things around me. I like documenting life’s moments, reflecting on experiences, and making every project feel purposeful and authentic.

I believe in human-centered design, meaningful experiences, and letting every project tell a story.
